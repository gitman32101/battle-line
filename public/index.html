📖 Rules Reference

Objective

Win 3 adjacent flags (Breakthrough) or any 5 flags (Envelopment). Each flag is won by the better 3-card formation on your side.

Each Turn

  • Play 1 card face-up at any flag (max 3 cards per flag per side)
  • Claim any flags you can prove you've already won
  • Draw 1 card from either deck

Formations (highest → lowest)

  • Wedge — same color, consecutive values (e.g. R3·R4·R5)
  • Phalanx — three of same value (e.g. 7·7·7)
  • Battalion Order — same color, any values
  • Skirmish Line — consecutive values, any color
  • Host — anything else; highest sum wins

Ties: the player who played their last card first loses the tie.

Claiming a Flag

You need 3 cards played and must prove your opponent cannot beat your formation using only cards still available (not yet on the board or discarded). Click a glowing flag token to claim it.

Tactics Cards

👑 Alexander / Darius
Wild card — any color & value 1–10. Resolved when claiming. Max 1 per player on the line.
🐴 Companion Cavalry
Counts as value 8 of any color. Choose color when playing.
🛡️ Shield Bearers
Any color, value 1, 2, or 3. Choose when playing.
🌫️ Fog
Played at a flag. Formations ignored — highest sum wins instead.
🪨 Mud
Played at a flag. Both sides need 4 cards to claim (not 3).
🔭 Scout
Draw 3 cards, then return 2 to any decks. Does not count as your draw for the turn.
↩️ Redeploy
Move one of your own cards from an unclaimed flag to another flag, or discard it.
⚡ Deserter
Discard one of your opponent's cards from an unclaimed flag.
🗡️ Traitor
Take one of your opponent's troop cards and place it on your side of any flag.

You may never have played more Tactics cards than your opponent + 1.
